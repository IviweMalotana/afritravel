The opportunity

Travel within Africa is booming — but the tools aren't built for it

  • Payments don't fit. Global platforms lean on cards; Africans pay with Mobile Money, USSD and local rails.
  • Inventory is thin. Local stays are under-listed and priced in the wrong currencies.
  • Visas are friction. Free-movement blocs (AfCFTA, ECOWAS, EAC, SADC) exist, but travellers don't know their rights and borders aren't digital.

The wedge

One platform that lists African stays, accepts Paystack & Mobile Money, and issues a digital Visa-Free Pass in partnership with governments — so booking a trip and crossing the border happen in one place.

54 markets7+ currenciesGov-partnered
The product

Four portals, one marketplace

🧳 Travellers

Search & book stays across Africa, pay with Paystack or Mobile Money, manage trips.

🪪 Visa-Free Pass

Register once, get an instantly-approved digital border pass with a scannable code.

🏨 Merchants

Hosts list properties, manage bookings & calendars, and receive payouts.

🛡️ Admin

Platform health: inventory, transactions, KYC, fraud & compliance oversight.

💳 Payments

Paystack (card/USSD/bank) + MTN/Airtel/M-Pesa Mobile Money, multi-currency.

🤝 Governments

Immigration partners verify passes — the rails for digital free movement.
